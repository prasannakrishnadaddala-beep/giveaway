How Prize Drops Work

Transparent, verifiable, and completely legal under Indian consumer law.

🛍️
Browse Drops
Explore active prize drop campaigns. Each campaign lists the product, entry price, and number of spots remaining.
💳
Reserve Your Spot
Pay a small entry fee via UPI. Your spot is reserved instantly. You can enter multiple spots to increase your chances.

When all spots are taken, the campaign closes. The selection algorithm runs automatically using a public SHA-256 seed.
